    Puzzle 4 deadline is closed!
    This was a very popular puzzle with several correct solutions!
    You start with 679 leftovers.
    From those, you make 679/7 = 97 make candles and no leftovers remain
    However, once you sold them, the customers will bring back their leftovers again!
    So you can make another 97/7 = 13 candles and will have 6 leftovers remaining
    Then, they bring back 13 leftovers. Along with the previous 6, you have 19 leftovers, enough for 2 new candles and 5 leftovers remaining
    Finally, when they bring back those two candles, you will have a total of 7 leftovers, enough for one more final candle to be crafted.

    So the answer is, you can make 97+13+2+1=113 candles!

    As a sidenote, the same number can be reached by simply dividing 679/6 =113,
    but thats not always a logically correct way to solve the puzzle as it would return 6/6=1 if you only started with 6 leftovers.
