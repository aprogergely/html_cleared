Still no correct solutions submitted!

This puzzle was combination of true-false statements, truth-and-lies.
Mr. Rhyme claimed he will decide which sack of coins to give you depending on your answer, so he does not yet know what you will be getting.
He also claimed to give you nothing if you are lying.

Under those terms, the correct answer is "You will not give me the small sack of coins".

If this statement is false, you get nothing because you were lying. But if you get nothing the statement is actually correct! That leads to a contradiction.

If the statement is correct, then he cannot give you the smaller sack, but it contains new information so he must give you something. Which means you are guaranteed to get the bigger sack of coins!
