The mission was a success! Now you can return and plant the fruit in your own city!
However, your resources are running low and you are aware somebody has been here shortly before you.
If it were a member of the opposing team, they'd return with the fruits before you do! There is but one way to solve both issues at once:
Descending down the cliffside! You can even see Shalour City nearby, a much faster stroll through the woods
instead of having to go through the maze again.
​

You see a large builder near the edge, where you could tie your rope.
The biggest problem with this is that your rope isnt quite long enough.
You're about 40 meters high up with only 30 meters of rope.

You do see another cliff about halfway down, where you could rest and tie another rope if you had one...
But unfortunately you don't.

Is there any way you could descend safely?​

Puzzle 11 deadline is closed!
This was a tough one but also a puzzle with several different solutions.
I have received 3 submissions total:

Devalue thought of unbraiding the rope and remaking it into a slightly longer rope.
This is a tedious task that needs some crafting skill, but depending on the thickness of the original rope
(which wasnt specified) it could work and still be faster than going back to the cave.

Dogparty has come up with multiple creative solutions, in particular trying to climb down while holding
both ends of the rope in your hands and not tying it at all so you can pull it down once you get to the
resting point. However I cannot give points for those as they still require you to jump several meters down, which is too risky.

5qwerty's solution may be the safest option, placing a lit candle next to the rock, close to the rope,
so that it would light the rope on fire after some time once the candle gets shorter and release the knot, making the rope fall down.

The correct solution also uses the candle in a similar way. You tie the rope in such a way that a 20m and
a 10m half is dangling down. You climb down the 20m half and once you reach 10m, you light the 10m half on fire.
Once the fire reaches the top, the knot gets undone and the other 20m half of the rope falls down to you.
